Authenticators avoid deepfakes by using technology and behavioral techniques. The following methods are used to identify deepfakes.

Layered approach

Layered approach is the most popular method that is used by authenticators to verify identities. AI-based forensics is particularly helpful for investigating flaws in media. For instance, they can tell a fake smile and mismatched lips movements.

Biometric Checks

Use liveness and biometrics checks can used to verify identities. For instance, a user may be asked to randomly turn the head or raise their hands. This can assist to confirm that a real person is behind a system rather than a manipulated ai video.

Metadata Use

The use of metadata can assist in determining where a media was located and whether someone has tampered with it. If there is no content of metadata, one can assume that it has been tampered with.

Human Verification

Humans are also used to verify the deepfakes. Calls are made in the verification process. The combination of human and ai strategies create a multi-layered security that protect against the deepfakes.
